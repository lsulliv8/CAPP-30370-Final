Who is Peeta Mellark?
Peeta Mellark is the male tribute from District 12 alongside Katniss in the 74th Hunger Games. A baker’s son, Peeta’s very charismatic personality proves very useful when he delivers a very convincing confession of his longtime love for Katniss. Throughout their experiences, Peeta grows very close to katniss and is very open about his belief that Katniss will win the Hunger Games.

Josh Hutcherson
Peeta Mellark is played by 21 year old actor Josh Hutcherson. Hutcherson, similar to Hunger Games co-star Jennifer Lawrence, originates from Kentucky. With other roles in The Polar Express, Bridge to Terabithia, and an old popular TV series ER, prior to playing Peeta Mellark was most known for his roles in RV and The Kids Are Alright. However, Hutcherson became infintely more famous after being cast as Peeta in the Hunger Games trilogy.
