Who is Katniss Everdeen?
Katniss is a 16-year-old girl from District 12 of Panem who lives with her mother and younger sister Prim. With food so scarce in District 12, Katniss hunts (against the rules) to provide for her family. At the reaping for the 74th Annual Hunger Games, Katniss’ sister Prim’s name is called, after which Katniss volunteers herself as female tribute from district 12. With intricate costumes designed to represent District 12, Katniss quickly gains a name for herself as the “Girl on Fire”.

Jennifer Lawrence
Katniss Everdeen is played by 23 year old actress Jennifer Lawrence. From Louisville Kentucky, Lawrence has also played roles in Silver Linings Playbook and House at the End of the Street. Despite previous Academy Aware and Golden Globe nominations for her role as Ree in Winter's Bone, Lawrence is best known for her role as Katniss Everdeen in the movie production of the Hunger Games trilogy.
