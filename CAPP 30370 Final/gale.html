Who is Gale Hawthorne?
An 18-year-old boy also from district 12, Gale Hawthorne is Katniss’ best friend and hunting partner. A very strong and handsome boy, Gale catches much attention from the girls of District 12, but has always been very close to Katniss. It’s not until after Katniss leaves for The Hunger Games and develops a very public and intimate relationship with Peeta Mellark, that Gale confronts his feelings for Katniss.

Liam Hemsworth
Gale Hawthorne is played by 23 year old actor Liam Hemsworth. Originally from Melbourne Australia, Hemsworth is proceeded in the movie industry by his two older brothers Luke and Chris. Also playing roles in Paranoia and Empire State, Liam was put on the map by his role in The Last Song with Miley Cyrus. However such fame has increased tenfold since landing the role as Gale Hawthorne in the Hunger Games movies.
